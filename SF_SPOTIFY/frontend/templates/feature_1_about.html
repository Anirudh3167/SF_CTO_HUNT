Feedback
Need:-
Why should the spotify users always listen only?
We are providing an option now for users to speak via Feedback.
Not only from user point of view but also form the spotify side it is very much helpful for the Spotify to know what the user actually needs.
How it Works:-
Spotify has its custom feedback section where it can create the form and ask the user about the spotify experience.
Surveys
Need:-
This section of spotify will act like a mediator between the survey company and the user.
How it works:-
A survey company will approach Spotify and made a contract with Spotify.

Let's assume that the survey company pays 0.5INR for each 10 survey reports.
Now the spotify will pay the user 20% of this 0.05INR for spending their time and filling the form.
This also encourages the web3 features.

Some details like name, email, phone no. is autofilled form spotify as it already stores the data.
